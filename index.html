About

        Nike, Inc., formerly 1964-78 Blue Ribbon Sports, American sportswear
        company headquartered in Beaverton, Oregon. It was founded in 1964 as
        Blue Ribbon Sports by Bill Bowerman, a track-and-field coach at the
        University of Oregon, and his former student Phil Knight. They opened
      their first retail outlet in 1966 and launched the Nike brand shoe in 1972.

Men
